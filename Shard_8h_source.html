60 /* A range of historical ledgers backed by a node store.
61  Shards are indexed and store `ledgersPerShard`.
62  Shard `i` stores ledgers starting with sequence: `1 + (i * ledgersPerShard)`
63  and ending with sequence: `(i + 1) * ledgersPerShard`.
64  Once a shard has all its ledgers, it is never written to again.
65 
66  Public functions can be called concurrently from any thread.
